StarrySwarm

This work is an art project that explores how different swarm algorithms, algorithms based off of collective intelligent systems in nature, can make art from inputted audio. I use Processing to visualize KANTS, PSO, and BOIDS and compare the outputs based on visual variability for different music genres and sounds. In my published thesis, I discuss how StarrySwarm, my implementation of BOIDS, was the most dynamic across movement and color and responsive to changes in sound.

Planet Music

This project uses touchdesigner and a mediapipe machine learning plugin to track hand movements, creating an animation of a distorted picture that reacts to music. One hand affects the size, rotation, and placement of the circle while the other hand affects the noise distortion and audio.

Headset Audio Translator

Image Playlist Generator

TV Show Logger

Full stack app using Android and TMDB API to track, rate, and log TV shows.

Wanting Smooth Air

Based on a randomly generated title prompt, "Wanting Smooth Air" inspired me to think about bodies, movement, and glitching. I gave some of my friends the prompts and had them arrange their hands in however way the word reminded them and drew on their hands. Wanting Smooth Air also made me think of the feeling of being trans, dysphoria, yearning, top surgery, smooth scars, growing, sprouting, trees, finally being able to breathe air.
